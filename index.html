We do not collect log data or personally identifiable information. However, in case of technical errors, standard system logs may be generated by your device or platform.

Cookies

This app does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Firebase services (such as Firestore) to store and retrieve question data. These services do not collect personal user information.

For more information, you can review Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of misuse of your personal information. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to ensure a secure experience.
